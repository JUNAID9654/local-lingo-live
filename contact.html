Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get updates about Local Lingo Live?
Subscribe to our newsletter at the bottom of this page to receive updates about new features, languages, and improvements. You can also follow our GitHub repository for technical updates.
How can I contribute to Local Lingo Live?
I welcome contributions! You can contribute by submitting language corrections, suggesting new features, or even contributing code on GitHub. Please reach out via the contact form with your ideas.
What is Local Lingo Live?
Local Lingo Live is a platform designed to help users learn and translate regional languages and dialects. It focuses on preserving linguistic diversity by making local languages accessible to everyone.
Is Local Lingo Live available for offline use?
Currently, Local Lingo Live requires an internet connection. However, we're exploring options for offline functionality in future updates.
